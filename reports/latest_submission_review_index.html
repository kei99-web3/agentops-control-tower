  7. Review reports/latest_submission_deadline_burndown.html for target submit timing and milestone order.
  8. Open reports/latest_next_approval_packet.html to decide the next explicit approval phrase.
  9. Open reports/latest_public_launch_snapshot.html to confirm the exact repo/video launch packet before approval.
  10. Open reports/latest_approval_consistency_audit.html to confirm stale Splunk-first guidance is not present.
  11. Open reports/latest_status_conflict_audit.html to confirm no stale failed status remains in JSON reports.
  12. Review reports/latest_control_tower.html and reports/latest_demo_tour.html for the story.
  13. Review Splunk evidence: local SPL proof, .spl package manifest, Splunk MCP command plan, proof brief, and prompt pack.
  14. Review Devpost final copy and URL validation.
  15. Only after explicit approval, proceed to public repo, video, optional live Splunk/MCP proof, and final Devpost submit gates.
